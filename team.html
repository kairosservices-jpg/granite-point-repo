Meet Our Team

Licensed PTs and PTAs with specialized training spanning women's health, orthopedics, persistent pain, vestibular disorders, and more.

Amanda Thome

PT, DPT • Sandpoint Clinic

Amanda graduated magna cum laude with her Doctorate in Physical Therapy from Columbia University. She specializes in creating unique, tailored interventions with a primary focus on dizziness, complex vestibular disorders, balance issues, and post-concussion recovery.

Bringing experience from top-ranked rehabilitation facilities, Amanda holds advanced dual certifications in Vestibular and Concussion therapy (AIB-VRC) and is an ImPACT-trained physical therapist. She is also a certified provider for BalanceWear, Parkinson Wellness Recovery (PWR!Moves), and possesses extensive training in craniosacral therapy and headache management. Outside the clinic, she enjoys training for marathons, hiking, and biking with her husband and dachshund, Lucy.

Alexis Creighton

PT, DPT • Athol Clinic

A Sandpoint native, Alexis earned her Doctor of Physical Therapy degree from George Fox University in 2023. She utilizes a holistic, bio-psycho-social approach—combining manual therapy with active treatment—to prioritize patient autonomy, education, and individualized care.

Alexis holds advanced certifications in fall prevention (Otago), Parkinson Wellness Recovery (PWR!Moves), and is an ImPACT-trained physical therapist for concussion care. She is also trained in blood flow restriction, modern manual therapy, and pelvic floor conditions. Outside the clinic, she loves golfing, paddleboarding, and hiking across North Idaho.

Kerby Andersen

PT, DPT • Sandpoint Clinic

An honors graduate from Rocky Mountain University of Health Professions, Kerby brings an athletic background as a former collegiate track and field sprinter and pole vaulter. He believes that every patient has unique needs, utilizing functional movements to create personalized plans of care.

Driven by a desire for lifelong learning, Kerby is a trained Dry Needling provider, former certified personal trainer, and high school track coach. He specializes in orthopedic conditions, injury prevention, strength and conditioning, and balance-related deficits to help active individuals get back to peak performance.

Vashti Graphenteen

PT, DPT • Sandpoint Clinic

Vashti earned her Doctorate in Physical Therapy from the University of South Dakota in 2013. She specializes in female pelvic rehabilitation, with a strong clinical focus on treating pelvic pain, bowel and bladder issues, pregnancy care, and postpartum recovery.
